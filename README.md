# Feline Understanding Study Companion

A project worked on across about eight months from February to September 2025, with additional fixes performed from October to November 2025.

The Feline Understanding Study Companion was a proof-of-concept of a desktop pet with studying features, such as timers and notepads.

This project may be reworked in the future in another engine, adding other external integrations and features.

Cat spritesheets: [SeethingSwarm](https://seethingswarm.itch.io/catset) on itch.io
